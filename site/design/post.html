🏰 Just finished my mega build after 6 months!

After exactly 187 days and over 500 hours of work, I've finally completed my massive medieval city project! This has been my passion project since the pandemic started, and I'm so excited to share it with all of you.

The city features:

  • A fully walled city with 4 distinct districts
  • Over 150 unique buildings including shops, homes, and workshops
  • A grand castle with throne room, barracks, and secret passages
  • Functional redstone mechanisms for gates and lighting
  • Custom terrain with rivers, forests, and mountains
  • An underground network of catacombs and dungeons

I've included a world download link at the bottom of this post for anyone who wants to explore it themselves. I'd love to hear your feedback and suggestions!

📥 World Download

You can download the world file here: Medieval_City_BuildMaster_v1.0.zip

Requirements: Minecraft 1.20.4, 8GB RAM recommended for optimal performance
